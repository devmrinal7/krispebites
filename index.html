About Us

We’re not about diet cardboard or masala explosions. Krispé Bites is that rare middle child — crunchy, tasty, and raised right. Our first creation, Coin Khakhra, brings bite-sized flavor packed fresh from Gujarat’s Mahila Gruh Udhyogs, where every snack is crafted with love, hygiene, and tradition. Not bland like “healthy” chips, not junky like the rest, just the Right Crunch with No Junk. Swad bhi, sehat bhi — bilkul ghar jaisa.
